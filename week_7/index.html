Biriyani

Bangladesh
Cook Time

1 hour 30 minutes

Image found here
Ingredients

  • 2 cups of short-grain rice.
  • 2lbs of bone-in beef.
  • 2tbps salt or to taste.
  • 2tbps cumin.
  • 1.5tbps corriander
  • 2tbps red chilli power
  • 1tbps tumeric
  • 0.5tsp nutmeg
  • 1tsp black pepper
  • 4 cardimons
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cinamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
Cook Directions
  • Cook 2 cups of rice, with 4 cups of water.
  • Marinate beef with the ingredients in red, and leave for 30 mins.
  • Cook the marinated beef on medium for about 1 hour.
  • Mix the cooked beef into the rice.
  • Enjoy!

Make sure to cook the meat all the way through
